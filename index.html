🍽️ How To Make Dosa

📝 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • ½ teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

🔪 Steps:

  1. Soak the ingredients

    Wash rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds. Soak them in water for 6–8 hours or overnight.

  2. Grind the batter

    Drain the water and grind everything into a smooth batter by adding water gradually.

  3. Fermentation

    Transfer the batter to a bowl, add salt, and let it ferment for 8–12 hours until it becomes slightly fluffy.

  4. Prepare the pan

    Heat a non-stick pan or tawa. Lightly grease it with oil.

  5. Spread the dosa

    Pour a ladle of batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin layer.

  6. Cook until crispy

    Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges. Cook until the bottom turns golden brown.

  7. Fold and serve

    Fold the dosa and serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.

💡 Tip:

  • For crispy dosa, spread the batter thin and cook on medium-high heat.

🥟 How To Make Samosa

📝 Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 4 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • ½ cup green peas
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for dough)
  • Oil (for frying)

🔪 Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough

    In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and a little oil. Add water gradually and knead into a firm dough. Let it rest for 20–30 minutes.

  2. Make the filling

    Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and green chilies. Add peas and mashed potatoes. Mix well.

  3. Add spices

    Add turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2–3 minutes and let the mixture cool.

  4. Shape the samosas

    Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each into an oval shape and cut it in half. Form a cone using one half.

  5. Fill the samosa

    Fill the cone with potato mixture and seal the edges using water.

  6. Fry the samosas

    Heat oil in a pan and fry the samosas on medium heat until golden brown and crispy.

  7. Serve hot

    Serve with chutney or ketchup.

💡 Tip:

  • Fry on medium-low heat for extra crispy and evenly cooked samosas.

🥟 How To Make Momos

📝 Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • Water (for dough)
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • ½ cup finely chopped carrots
  • ½ cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1–2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oil

🔪 Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough

    In a bowl, mix flour and a pinch of salt. Add water gradually and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 20–30 minutes.

  2. Prepare the filling

    Heat oil in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.

  3. Cook the vegetables

    Add chopped cabbage, carrots, and onions. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.

  4. Add seasoning

    Add salt and soy sauce. Mix well and cook for another minute. Let the filling cool.

  5. Shape the momos

    Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into thin circles.

  6. Fill and fold

    Place a spoonful of filling in the center and fold the edges to seal (pleated or half-moon shape).

  7. Steam the momos

    Place them in a steamer and steam for 10–12 minutes until cooked.

  8. Serve hot

    Serve with spicy red chutney or sauce.

💡 Tip:

  • Do not overcook while steaming, or momos may become dry.

🍜 How To Make Noodles

📝 Ingredients:

  • 200 grams noodles
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (carrot, cabbage, capsicum)
  • 2–3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 1–2 green chilies (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

🔪 Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles

    Boil water in a pot, add noodles and cook according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.

  2. Heat the pan

    Heat oil in a wok or pan on high flame.

  3. Sauté garlic and chilies

    Add chopped garlic and green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

  4. Cook the vegetables

    Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes (keep them slightly crunchy).

  5. Add sauces

    Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and ketchup. Mix well.

  6. Add noodles

    Add boiled noodles and toss everything together properly.

  7. Season and mix

    Add salt if needed and mix well for another minute.

  8. Serve hot

    Serve immediately while hot.

💡 Tip:

  • Cook on high flame for authentic street-style noodles flavor.

🥭 How To Make Mango Ice Cream

📝 Ingredients:

  • 2 cups ripe mango pulp
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • ½ cup condensed milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla essence (optional)
  • Sugar (optional, as per taste)

🔪 Steps:

  1. Prepare mango pulp

    Peel and chop ripe mangoes. Blend them into a smooth pulp.

  2. Whip the cream

    In a bowl, whip the fresh cream until it becomes light and fluffy.

  3. Mix ingredients

    Add mango pulp and condensed milk to the whipped cream. Mix gently.

  4. Add flavor

    Add vanilla essence (optional) and mix well.

  5. Adjust sweetness

    Taste the mixture and add sugar if needed.

  6. Freeze the mixture

    Pour the mixture into an airtight container and freeze for 6–8 hours.

  7. Serve chilled

    Scoop and serve the mango ice cream cold.

💡 Tip:

  • Use sweet and ripe mangoes for best flavor — you may not even need extra sugar.

🎂 How To Make White Cake with Chocolate Syrup

📝 Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter (softened)
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

For Chocolate Syrup:

  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter

🔪 Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven

    Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a baking pan.

  2. Prepare the batter

    In a bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add eggs and vanilla

    Add eggs one by one and mix well. Add vanilla essence.

  4. Mix dry ingredients

    In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.

  5. Combine everything

    Gradually add dry ingredients and milk to the batter. Mix until smooth.

  6. Bake the cake

    Pour batter into the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Prepare chocolate syrup

    In a pan, mix cocoa powder, sugar, and milk. Cook on low heat while stirring. Add butter and mix until smooth.

  8. Add syrup on cake

    Let the cake cool, then pour warm chocolate syrup over the top.

  9. Serve and enjoy

    Slice and serve your delicious white cake with chocolate syrup.

💡 Tip:

  • Let the cake cool completely before adding syrup for a perfect finish.
